Philosophy

Principles that respect how researchers work

Speed of thought

Type anything and it becomes a note. No forms, no clicks — just capture.

Source-first

Every note carries bibliographic context. Citations are generated automatically.

Session continuity

Set a source and previous notes link to it. Your workflow adapts to your thinking.

No lock-in

Data lives in PostgreSQL, exports to clean Markdown. No proprietary formats.

Features

Everything for structured, citable research notes

Inline notes

Just type — text becomes a note with automatic page and timestamp detection.

Source management

Books, articles, videos — full metadata with MLA-style citations.

Smart tagging

Organize with tags. Add, remove, and browse with tab completion.

Full-text search

Find any note instantly across your entire knowledge base.

Markdown export

Export by source, tag, or author to portable Markdown files.

Multi-user

Authentication with invite codes. Your team, your knowledge base.

Architecture

Simple by design. Robust by nature.

Snippets API

FastAPI with raw SQL. No ORM — clarity and performance by choice.

PostgreSQL

Battle-tested storage with full-text search and multi-tenant isolation.
